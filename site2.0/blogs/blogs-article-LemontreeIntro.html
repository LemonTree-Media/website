LemonTree Media - Introducing Interactive Video

Hi there. Let us introduce ourselves.

We are LemonTree Media, an interactive media startup based in New York City. Our goal is to usher in a new era of advertising by utilizing interactive video ads to help brands meet their needs.

Introducing Interactive Video

There are thousands of content creators all across the internet seeking to get their voices heard. It’s easy to become just another voice in the crowd. Wanting to use a different, more meaningful way of brand marketing, we want to usher in interactive video advertising.

What exactly are interactive video ads? Think traditional video ads embedded with elements such as embedded buttons, branching storylines, polls and quizzes. By prompting the customer to interact with the video ad, we boost audience engagement and click-through rates. Interactive video ads forge a stronger connection between your brand and your customer, allowing you to satisfy your company’s marketing needs.

Let us show you an example. Our client was Gramercy Gift Guide: a whimsical and smart luxury gift guide dedicated to making the entire gift giving process fast, flawless, and fun. For our video ad, titled “A Special Gift”, we had actors tell stories about the most precious gifts they had ever received. In this shot, Sara shows to the camera a ring that her mother gifted to her.

In the next shot, we move to a close-up shot of her hand and embed a non-intrusive button that the audience can click to learn more about Sara’s story.

Upon clicking the button, we get a clean pop-up that introduces the audience to Sara’s story and prompts them to click another button, “Find out more."

Clicking this button takes the audience to Gramercy Gift Guide’s website, where we hope to convert them from just a passive watcher to an active buyer of the product. In this entire process we use a seamless interactive experience. Once the video starts, instead of using just visuals to pique the audience’s curiosity about the brand, we actively invite them to visit the company’s website by using interactive elements, and activate their purchasing decision.

Rather than just talking at your customer, interactive video immerses the viewer in your content, driving brand engagement and sustaining brand recall. Interactive content generates 2x the conversions of passive content, and 5x more page views than regular content. And it’s gaining in popularity, too: 88% of marketers claim that interactive content helps them stand out from the competition. So what are you waiting for?
