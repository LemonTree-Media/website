Why is interactive video going to be the future of storytelling

Introduction

Interactive videos are videos embedded onto websites using popular web technologies. They are interactive because there are buttons that viewers can click on to decide the outcome of a story and answer questions. Interactive video has been around for a while, and have been used by bigger brands such as Deloitte and Honda in their original content marketing campaigns. But why is it not gaining traction? In this article, we will be going over the prevalent interactive formats out there and the reason to integrate interactive videos in your exisitng marketing initiatives to engage your audience more.

A fish tank is just interactive television for cats.

-Oliver Gaspirtz, A Treasury of Pet Humor

It starts with a mirror in an apartment lobby. It inspires us to explore what we do when we look into the mirror. We see reflections of ourselves, but what do we feel? Nervousness, excitement, happiness. The film captures a supposedly awkward moment at the apartment lobby and stages a lovely encounter between a man and a woman. Throughout the duration of the film, we explored a range of emotions, letting the characters discover their true reflections. We selected the apartment lobby with limited natural lighting. By capturing the entire story in one camera shot and keeping the background music as only the surrounding sound, the film brings the viewers into experiencing the moment.
